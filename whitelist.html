The Whitelist

Our minecraft server has a whitelist, this means that you won't be able to join until an admin adds your username. This process is typically quick and painless; if you've managed to register for courses at UVic you can figure it out.

Need help? No problem. Join our discord server and message one of the admins!

Getting added

Getting on the whitelist is easy! Follow the template below in an email to whitelist@uvicmc.club with the subject line "whitelist". 

Full Name: your name
Minecraft Username: your minecraft username
(Optional) Discord Name: your discord username
You must send the email from your netlink@uvic.ca email address as this is how we verify that you actually go to UVic.

Why a whitelist?

Since the server is nearly 100% vanilla (no factions, claimed chunks, per-player plots, etc.), we need to trust players to be mature and follow the rules. A whitelist provides a degree of accountability.

About Us

The UVic Minecraft Club is a place to make friends, play some games, and have a chill time. Our main thing is a whitelisted minecraft survival server that's been running 24/7 since February of 2019. So far we've had over 250 unique players!

Want to get involved? Join our discord server or facebook page, and follow the whitelist instructions. We'd love to meet you :).
